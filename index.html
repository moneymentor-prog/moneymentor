Your Favorite Neighborhood Cafe

Welcome to Steamy Mugs, an adorable, tiny cafe nestled in the heart of Tilak Nagar, Chembur. We're the perfect nook for a hot cup of tea with a side of great conversations and even better company.

Whether you're looking for a quick chai break, a cozy spot to work, or a lively hangout with friends, our warm atmosphere and friendly service make every visit special. We pride ourselves on serving fresh, delicious food at prices that won't break the bank.
